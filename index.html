I am currently a first year Ph.D. student at Tsinghua University, Guangdong, in the SSR group (supervised by Prof. Wenbo Ding and Prof. Xiaojun Liang).
My current research focuses on embodied intelligence and MLLMs, with a special emphasis on embodied navigation and embodied foundation models.

Before joining Tsinghua University, I received my M.Phil. in Microelectronics in 2025 from The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), supervised by Prof. Renjing Xu and Prof. Xinyu Chen. I obtained my B.Eng. in Electronics Information in 2023 from Beijing Institute of Technology, and conducted a research internship in 2023 at the Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. Fangwen Yu and Prof. Luping Shi. I was also a research intern at BAAI and Xiaomi EV, supervised by Dr. Xiaoshuai Hao and Dr. Long Chen.

My research interests include:

  • Embodied Intelligence
  • Embodied Foundation Model
  • Embodied Navigation
  • Embodied Manipulation
  • Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Model
